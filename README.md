# Sales-Dashboard
# Project Overview

The dataset includes store-level, customer-level, and product-level information that enables end-to-end analysis of sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends.

# Dataset Description

Column Name-Description

StoreID-Unique identifier for each store

ProductID-Unique identifier for each product

Product Name-Name of the product sold

Country-Country where the sale occurred

Region-Region of the store (e.g., North, West, etc.)

City-City of the store

State-State of the store

Unit Price-Price per unit of the product

Customer Name-Name of the customer

Age-Customer age

Gender-Customer gender

Loyalty Level-Customer loyalty tier 

Stock-Units available in inventory

Category-Product category

Quantity-Units sold

Payment Type-Mode of payment

Discount-Discount applied to the order

Store Name-Name of the store

# key insight

1.Sports and Electronics categories consistently outperform others, indicating strong customer demand.

2.Majority of customers fall within the age group 45-60, contributing the highest purchase volume.

3.Store type affects sales patterns—online stores tend to process more orders with smaller quantities.

4.Morrisbury and Philipchester show noticeable performance differences; certain cities drive the largest share of total revenue.

5.Bean,Jones and park store contributes the largest share of total revenue compared to all other stores, making it a critical performer in the business.
